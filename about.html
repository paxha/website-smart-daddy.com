OUR STORY

Built by parents who wanted better for their own kids

SMART DADDY started with a simple wish: to hand a child a device without worrying about ads, trackers, or unsafe content — and to make those early screen moments genuinely help them learn.

Today that’s an online kindergarten for ages 2–6 and a growing family of kid-safe apps, all under one brand you can trust. One trusted home for a child’s first, most important years online.
